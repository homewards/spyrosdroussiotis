Spyros Droussiotis






Spyros Droussiotis was born in Cyprus, he got interest in Photography from early age. After studying Photography in Athens, he moved to Berlin and began his career as a fashion photographer. His latest editorial was published in Numero magazine. Since 2016 he is located between Germany, France & Cyprus.
